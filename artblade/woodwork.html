About the Safe

This wooden safe features a fully functional and 100% wooden locking mechanism.
Base: walnut
Tumblers & slider: pecan
Completed 2013

The locking mechanism

The mechanism for locking the box is fairly simple in concept. Each external dial is attached to a slightly smaller internal dial containing a notch. Rotating the outer dials thus turns the internal dials. When the right combination is in place, all the notches on the internal dials align, allowing the internal locking plate to slide out of the "teeth" on the safe's lid, and allowing the lid to be lifted.

Check out the last page of the designs to see a detailed diagram of the mechanism.
